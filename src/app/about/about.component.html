About Us

My Flix is a new platform created for independent content creators from filmmakers to podcasters. My Flix is dedicated to linking independent artists directly to their audience as well as a new audience through our extensive network viewers from all over the world. Your dollars go directly to these independent artists that make so many sacrifices to entertain us all. This platform premieres and showcases the newest and hottest high-quality content from all over the world in one spot... right here on My Flix.
